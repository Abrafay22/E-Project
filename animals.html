Animals

Lion

The lion is known as the king of the jungle. It is a large, muscular cat with a majestic mane.

Elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth, known for their intelligence and strong social bonds.

Panda

Pandas are lovable black-and-white bears that primarily eat bamboo and are native to China.

Tiger

The tiger is the largest species of the cat family. It is known for its powerful build and striking orange-and-black stripes.
